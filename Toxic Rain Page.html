Toxic Rain in C++ OpenGL - 2020 to 2021

As part of the Advanced games technology coursework requirements, I have made a game project using OpenGL C++ based on my own knowledge and content taught during Labs and Lectures. The title of the game is called Toxic Rain and this name was decided based on the theme that I had in mind. The theme of my game is post zombie apocalyptic theme similarly to already made titles such as Resident Evil franchise and Fallout. The theme was achieved by adding a skybox texture and terrain and adding minimal lighting. The genre of my game is a 3rd Person FPS taking inspiration from The division Tom Clancy's and Resident Evil 4/5/6 allowing the player to move around and look around with the camera positioned behind the character model's shoulder.

The project fulfilled all requirements and the list of requirements are as followed:

  • Creation of Primitive-based objects using vertices and X,Y,Z co-ordinates
  • Addition of Audio
  • HUD
  • Camera Technique(3rd Person or 1st Person)
  • Inclusion of Mesh-based objects
  • Logically used lighting effects (Spotlight, pointlight, directional light)
  • Special FX (Camera shaking, Fog etc...)
  • NPC and AI (Pathfinding or FSM)
  • Gameplay Elements(Powerups, Timer, Score system, gunplay)
